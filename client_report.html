What was the most challenging part of this project for you? Were you able to overcome it?

The most challenging part was making sure the website I did as a lead developer was matching the wireframe, which required asking the client a few questions. I was able to overcome that by communicating and being proactive.

What did you enjoy most about this project?

What I enjoyed the most was how it replicated a real world scenario and gave me an opportunity to experience all the roles in the development of a website. I also practiced communicating and being a team player. It was a really fun experience.

What would you do differently if you could do the project again?

I would start doing my junior developer roles sooner, that would have allowed me more time to communicate with my lead developer.

Do you feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this experience? Explain.

I feel more confident. After this project I was able to get a taste of how it would be to work in a team environment, solve problems, communicate, and be an effective part of a team. It was also good practice to have different roles in different websites. The experience of starting with the wireframe and ending with the finished website was very cool. I really enjoyed going through the whole process.
